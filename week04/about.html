The In-Between

Liminal spaces are often locations of transition: the point between 'what was' and 'what's next'. These spaces evoke a sense of unease because they appear familiar, yet somehow out of place. Many people often associiate these images with childhood. For example stopping at an empty gas station on a long car ride. They are images that connect us to share memories.
